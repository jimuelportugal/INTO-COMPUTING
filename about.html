Welcome to Frost Shake!



At Frost Shake, we’re all about delivering refreshing, delicious shakes that hit the spot. Whether you’re in the mood for something creamy, sweet, or a bit of both, we’ve got just the right flavor to satisfy your cravings.

Our Story

Frost Shake was started by Jimuel L. Portugal, a 1st-year Computer Science student, out of his love for shakes. One day, craving a shake but unable to find a shop nearby, he decided to make his own. What began as a small idea quickly grew into something much bigger as people loved the taste and shared their positive reviews.

Today, Frost Shake has grown into a thriving business, offering delivery services so you can enjoy your favorite shakes from the comfort of your home.

Thank you for supporting Frost Shake – where every sip is made with care and passion!
